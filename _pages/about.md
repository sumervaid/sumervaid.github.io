Welcome to my homepage. I am a postdoctoral research associate at Harvard Business School. Previously, I earned my Ph.D. and M.A. in media psychology at Stanford. Before that, I earned a B.A. and M.A in Psychology, and a graduate certificate in Computational Social Science from the University of Chicago.

In my research, I study psychological fit through the lens of digital media in myriad different contexts, ranging from markets to organizations. 

In my free time, I like to do high-intensity interval training and obsess over the perfect nitro cold brew coffee. Before becoming a computational psychologist, I spent much of my life dreaming about becoming a cosmologist, with a particular interest in black hole creation.
